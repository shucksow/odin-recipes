Lamb Kleftiko

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 kg (3.3 lbs) lamb shoulder or leg
  • 4 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • 1 tbsp dried thyme
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 large tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 cup chicken or vegetable broth
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste

  2. Mix garlic, oregano, thyme, paprika, olive oil, and lemon juice in a bowl.
  3. Rub the mixture over the lamb.
  4. Place onions in a roasting pan, then place lamb on top.
  5. Arrange tomatoes around the lamb.
  6. Pour broth around the lamb.
  7. Season with salt and pepper.
  8. Cover tightly with foil and roast for 3-4 hours until tender.
  9. Remove foil, increase oven to 200째C (400째F), and roast for 15-20 minutes until golden brown.
  10. Let it rest, then serve with sides like roasted vegetables or potatoes.

Enjoy your easy and delicious Lamb Kleftiko!
