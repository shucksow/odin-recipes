Chicken Shawarma

Ingredients:

  • 500g (1 lb) boneless, skinless chicken thighs or breasts, thinly sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp ground paprika
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper (optional, for added heat)
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Pita bread or flatbread, for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix together minced garlic, ground cumin, paprika, turmeric, coriander, cinnamon, cayenne pepper (if using), lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and black pepper to form a marinade.
  2. Add the thinly sliced chicken to the marinade, ensuring it's well coated. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or ideally up to 2 hours to allow the flavors to develop.
  3. Preheat a grill pan or skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Once the pan is hot, add the marinated chicken slices in a single layer. Cook for 5-6 minutes on each side, or until the chicken is fully cooked and nicely charred.
  5. While the chicken is cooking, warm the pita bread or flatbread.
  6. Once the chicken is done, remove it from the pan and let it rest for a few minutes.
  7. To assemble the chicken shawarma wraps, place a generous portion of the cooked chicken onto each warmed pita bread. Add your desired toppings such as sliced tomatoes, lettuce, red onion, cucumber, and sauces like tahini or yogurt sauce.
  8. Roll up the pita bread tightly around the filling to form a wrap.
  9. Serve the chicken shawarma wraps immediately and enjoy!

This recipe yields flavorful and juicy chicken shawarma that's perfect for wrapping in pita bread or flatbread along with your favorite toppings and sauces.
